About Us

The Guru Nanak Dev DSEU campus in Rohini, Delhi, is a constituent campus of the Delhi Skill and Entrepreneurship University (DSEU). It offers a variety of diploma courses, particularly in engineering fields, and is known for its focus on practical, industry-relevant skills. The campus is located in Sector-15, Rohini, and provides facilities like a library and labs. .

With a diverse student body from all 50 states and over 100 countries, we foster an inclusive environment that encourages intellectual growth and personal development.

Our Mission

DSEU's mission is to provide skill-based education, foster an entrepreneurial mindset, and create a bridge between education and employment. Its vision is to accord dignity to all skills, cultivate an entrepreneurial spirit, and nurture industry-ready talent.
